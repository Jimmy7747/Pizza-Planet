    Our Story

    Pizza Planet opened in 2045, founded by an alien couple who came down to Earth in 2025. It initially started out as a small pop-up in Binghamton, but they instantaneously became a hit because of their unique pizza combinations and flavor explosions from outer space! There's no other pizza like Pizza Planet, our super secret alien family pizza recipe has been passed down for generations and we are glad to be able to share it with humans on Earth.

    Although the alien couple retired and went back to their home planet, MIS, their Pizza Planet legacy continues to be carried out by their 25 grandalienchildren.
